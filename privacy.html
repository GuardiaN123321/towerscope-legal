Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

TowerScope ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

With your permission, we access your device's GPS location to show nearby cell towers and calculate distances. Location data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Device Information

We collect basic device information (device model, OS version, app version) for diagnostics and improving app performance.

2.3 Network Information

On Android devices, we may access cellular network information (carrier, signal strength, network type) to provide network info features. This data is processed locally.

2.4 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics (screen views, feature usage) to improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.5 Purchase Data

When you subscribe to Premium, purchase transactions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not have access to your payment details.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not track your location history
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising targeting

4. Data Storage

All user preferences, speed test results, and search history are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do not maintain user accounts or cloud storage for personal data.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Mapbox — for map rendering (subject to Mapbox Privacy Policy)
  • OpenCelliD — for cell tower data (publicly available data)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for subscription management
  • Expo — for app updates and diagnostics

These services may collect their own data as described in their respective privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited data we handle. Since most data is stored locally on your device, your device's own security features provide the primary protection.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke location permissions at any time in device settings
  • Clear all locally stored data by uninstalling the App
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

9. European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on your consent (location access) and our legitimate interest in improving the App (anonymous analytics).
  • Right of Access: You may request information about the data we process.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your data by uninstalling the App (locally stored) or contacting us.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may revoke permissions in device settings at any time.
  • Right to Data Portability: Since data is stored locally, it is already under your control.
  • Right to Object: You may contact us to object to data processing.
  • Data Protection Authority: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your jurisdiction.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by a new "Last updated" date.
