Frequently Asked Questions

How does Tower Scope find cell towers?

Tower Scope uses the OpenCelliD database with over 5 million cell tower locations worldwide. The data is displayed on an interactive map powered by Mapbox, allowing you to explore towers by location, network type, and operator.

Why doesn't the speed test work?

Make sure you have an active internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular). The speed test measures your current download and upload speeds. If results seem unusual, try testing again in a few moments.

How do I filter towers by network type?

Use the filter options on the map screen to select specific network types: 2G (GSM), 3G (UMTS), 4G (LTE), or 5G (NR). You can also filter by operator name.

Is my location data shared?

No. Your location is processed locally on your device and is never sent to our servers or shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. Your subscription should be restored automatically. If not, try restarting the app.

The app is not showing towers in my area

Tower coverage depends on the OpenCelliD database. Some regions may have less data available. Try zooming out to see nearby towers, or use the search function to find towers by country or operator.
